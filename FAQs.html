What is Grocery Go?

Grocery Go is a mobile and convenience online store offering you immediate dispatch of a wide range of fresh produce

What is your trading hours?

Orders placed Mon-Sat before 7:30PM & Sunday before 5:30PM receive same-day delivery.


Is there a minimum order value to checkout?

Yes, we have a minimum cart sub-total (before delivery fees) of R 150.00 in order to checkout.
